# Music Attributions

All bundled music is public domain or openly licensed.

- **Amazing Grace** — Trad. American melody 'New Britain'; words John Newton (1725-1807). Public domain. ABC: John Chambers collection.
- **Auld Lang Syne** — Trad. Scots (Burns, 1788), melody 'Can Ye Labour Lea'. Public domain. ABC after R. L. Walker (John Chambers collection).
- **Danny Boy** — Trad. Irish 'Londonderry Air'; 'Danny Boy' words F. Weatherly (1910). Public domain. ABC: John Chambers collection.
- **Deck the Hall** — Old Welsh Air ("Nos Galan"); ABC from "The Everyday Song Book" (1927), transcribed by John Chambers, trillian.mit.edu
- **Frere Jacques** — Traditional French round. Public domain. ABC from folkinfo.org (Singing Together, BBC Publications, 1951), via abcnotation.com.
- **Für Elise** — Beethoven, Bagatelle in A minor 'Für Elise' (1810). Public domain. ABC transcription (F. Nordberg, public domain).
- **Greensleeves** — Traditional English (c. 1580). Public domain. ABC from The Session (CC0).
- **Happy Birthday** — Melody by Patty S. Hill and Mildred J. Hill (public domain since 2016); ABC from John Chambers collection, trillian.mit.edu (thisoldtractor mirror)
- **Jingle Bells** — James Lord Pierpont, 'Jingle Bells' (1857). Public domain. ABC from the John Chambers collection (trillian.mit.edu).
- **Lightly row** — German folk tune. Public domain. ABC transcribed by Frank Nordberg, musicaviva.com, via abcnotation.com.
- **London Bridge** — Traditional English game song, "The Everyday Song Book" (1927). Public domain. ABC transcribed by John Chambers (trillian.mit.edu), via abcnotation.com.
- **Mary Had a Little Lamb** — Traditional (England); ABC from musicaviva.com via John Chambers collection (trillian.mit.edu)
- **Michael Row the Boat Ashore** — Traditional African-American spiritual. Public domain. ABC transcribed by Frank Nordberg (musicaviva.com), via the John Chambers collection.
- **Minuet in G Major** — Petzold, Minuet in G (BWV Anh. 114), formerly attr. J. S. Bach. Public domain. ABC ed. AW (SPUDS).
- **Molly Malone** — Trad. Irish ('Cockles and Mussels'). Public domain. ABC from O'Neill's Music of Ireland (1903) via V. Brennan.
- **Ode to Joy** — Beethoven, 'Ode to Joy' (Symphony No. 9, 1824). Public domain. ABC from the John Chambers collection.
- **Oh Susanna** — Stephen C. Foster, 'Oh! Susanna' (1848). Public domain. ABC transcribed by Frank Nordberg (musicaviva.com), via the John Chambers collection.
- **Old MacDonald Had A Farm** — Traditional English. Public domain. ABC from Paul Hardy's Songs Tunebook (pghardy.net, CC BY-NC-SA), via abcnotation.com.
- **Row, Row, Row Your Boat** — Traditional round; ABC from John Chambers collection (trillian.mit.edu)
- **Scarborough Fair** — Trad. English ballad. Public domain. ABC transcr. Frank Nordberg (musicaviva.com).
- **This Old Man** — Traditional. Public domain. ABC transcribed by Frank Nordberg (musicaviva.com), via the John Chambers collection.
- **Twinkle, Twinkle Little Star** — Traditional kids' song; ABC from John Chambers collection (trillian.mit.edu, R.L. Walker abc/twinkle)
- **We Wish You a Merry Christmas** — Trad. English carol (West Country). Public domain. ABC via folkinfo.org.
- **When the saints go marching in** — American spiritual, traditional. Public domain. ABC transcribed by Frank Nordberg, musicaviva.com, via abcnotation.com.
- **Yankee Doodle** — Traditional (c. 1730). Public domain. ABC from the John Chambers collection (trillian.mit.edu).
